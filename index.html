Here are some of the most common repairs that we encounter:

Broken Springs

Broken garage door springs are common issue that can render you door inoperable. Springs are under high tension and support the weight of the door, making them prone to wear and tear over time. When a spring breaks, you may notice the door becoming heavy or difficult to lift manually. It's crucial to address broken springs promptly as attempting to fix them yourself can be dangerous due to the high tension involved. Professional garage door technicians have the knowledge and tools to safely replace broken springs, restoring your door's functionality and ensuring smooth operation.

Malfunctioning Garage Door Opener

A malfunctioning garage door opener can be frustrating and inconvenient. Issues with the opener can manifest as the door not responding to remote or keypad commands, making unusal noises during operation, or operating inconsistently. The problem could be due to electrical issues, damaged wiring, faulty sensors, or problems with the opener itself. Troubleshooting the opener may involve checking and replacing batteries, inspecting wiring connections, adjusting the settings, or repairing/replacing the opener unit. Garage door repair professionals have the expertise to diagnose and fix opener-related issues, restoring the convenience and reliability of your garage door system.

Misaligned Tracks

Misaligned garage door tracks can cause the door to become stuck, operate unevenly, or make grinding noises during operation. Tracks can become misaligned due to regular use, accidental impact, or general wear and tear. Common causes include loose hardware, bent tracks, or shifted mounting brackets. Repairing misaligned tracks typically involves carefully realigning them using a level, tightening loose hardware, and ensuring the tracks are properly secured and parallel. Professional technicians can quickly assess track alignment issues and perform the necessary adjustments to ensure smooth and safe door movement.

While these are three common fixes, it's important to note that garage door issues can vary, and the specific repair required will depend on the underlying problem. It's always recommended to consult a professional garage door repair service to accurately diagnose and address any issues with your garage door.
